Dr. Bonnie Fergie

Dermatologist

Dr Bonnie Fergie is a leading, experienced consultant dermatologist in Sydney, practicing across all areas of dermatology.

Entering specialist dermatology training early in her medical career, Bonnie brings over a decade of clinical dermatology experience, having treated tens of thousands of patients impacted by Australia’s unforgiving conditions.

Originally from country Western Australia, Bonnie is well known among the patient and referring doctor community for her expertise and kind patient manner.

Bonnie is the founder of new Australian skincare brand Bon Elliot, launching in March 2026, bringing her informed perspective on how the skin’s physiology defines its outer form.

Specialist training / publications

Bonnie undertook specialist dermatology training in New South Wales at St Vincent’s Hospital Sydney, The Skin Hospital Sydney, Canberra Hospital, The Royal Newcastle Hospital / John Hunter Hospital and The Orange Base Hospital.

Bonnie is a published author in peer-reviewed journals and has presented at national and international dermatology conferences.

Qualifications / memberships

  • MBBS, FACD
  • Fellow of Australasian College of Dermatologists
  • International Fellow of American Academy of Dermatology
  • Women’s Dermatologic Society

Practice

Bonnie currently practice by appointment at Drummoyne Dermatology.
